Denny Blaine Park Community Meeting

Sunday, 4/27/25:
We will be hosting a community meeting to discuss what's been going on at the beach, what's coming, and a Q&A period to discuss beachgoers' concerns and ideas. There will be an in-person meeting at 1:30pm, as well as a virtual component at 7:30pm, at https://us05web.zoom.us/j/84352039344.

Note: other than Q&A, the content of both meetings will be the same; there's just an in-person and a remote session to maximize access. No need to attend both.

We expect a lot of folks there that day \(it's also just spring\): Use public transit, carpool, bike, or:
