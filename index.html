Lawsuit Update

7/16/25:
An injunctive order has been filed by the judge in the "Denny Blaine For All" lawsuit against the city alleging nuisance at Denny Blaine Park, requiring the city take immediate steps to abate nuisance at the park.

The Friends of Denny Blaine Park, in coordination with park users, legal counsel, and city agencies, has drafted such an abatement plan, and urges the city to adopt this plan to address the requirements of the court order while preserving legal park use.
